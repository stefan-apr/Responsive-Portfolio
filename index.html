About


Hi! My name is Stefan Apreutesei, and I’m a software developer. My journey to this point has been an unconventional, but interesting and fun experience. I started off by taking as many Computer Science courses as I could at the University of Washington, but ultimately earned my degree in Earth Sciences with a GIS focus.


Looking for more opportunities to study Computer Science, I went on to complete a postgraduate Web Development bootcamp with the University of Washington. This environment put into perspective what software development, particularly as part of a team, is like. The coursework prepared me well in modern web development technologies; I greatly enjoyed making our team projects take shape, and I’m proud of the portfolio I’ve put together since.


Now searching for my next step along the path, I’m looking forward to contributing towards writing impactful software in the workforce.
